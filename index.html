Biography

I am 35 years old and an aspiring programmer. Currently I am living in Kirkland, Washington, though I have lived in many places in the US, including Alaska, Maine, Florida, Arizona and a few places in between. In my free time (which has become rather rare lately) I like to read, play video games and guitar, take care of my cats and go for walks.
